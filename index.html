DESIGN STYLE

From a simple single-tone style to a variety of two-tone looks to diamond stitch leather seats, Katzkin gives you many options for creating that special personalized design. LEATHERS & Specialty Interiors

KATZKIN®

AUTOMOTIVE LEATHER

Our premium leather is available in 60 colors, each designed to match original factory interior colors perfectly, across all makes and models. If you choose a two-tone option, the tow-tone colors will be placed on the front driver and passenger as well as the outer seats in the rear. Note that the middle rear seat will be a single-tone color as this is typical with factory designs.

STICHING,PIPING & PERFORATION

The simple application of contrasted stitching or piping accents to your leather trimmed seat design can add energy and character to your custom design.

Perforations can be added to any Katzkin Automotive Leather to add visual interest and dimension to seat designs.
